BIM LEVEL 2

In addition to highly qualified and skilled staff, Cowal Design is served by state-of-the-art IT systems benefiting from a high-speed internet connection and fully integrated software solutions and cloud storage. It provides a fully integrated knowledge environment, therefore providing easily shared project information both in terms of briefing data and deliverable information. We make use of 3D civil and structural analysis and design packages such as TEKLA and REVIT for the design and detailing of building frames, and WINDES and Civils 3D for Drainage and Infrastructure design. Project integration using BIM is more of the norm than exception. REVIT software is the defacto standard for structural models, used by both Architects and Engineers. The creation and sharing of REVIT models has been adopted on a number of primary school projects and we are currently working on BIM Level 2 project delivery.

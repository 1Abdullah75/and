About Us





Cowal Design, founded in 2005, has developed to be one of the most dynamic multidisciplinary engineering consultancies delivering projects throughout the UK and Ireland. We specialise in civil and structural designs, temporary works and infrastructure.

Our teams of civil and structural engineers, working across four offices deliver projects for private and public sector clients alike.

From inauguration, Cowal have set down the following core values:

  • Innovative design
  • Joined up thinking with all members of design teams
  • Integrated civil and structural design solutions
  • Close working relationship with client to facilitate the ideal end result
As consultants, we offer Civil and Structural Engineering Consultancy along with a range of supplementary services that surpass client expectations. Attention to detail is at the core of our approach, leading to a better thought out and fully resolved built environment.
We start with a thorough analysis of your needs and work forward from there. A full audit of environmental goals and particularly sustainability is an essential part of our approach and working processes.

The company directors act as lead on all projects, ensuring continuity throughout. Working alongside are a team of specialists & consultants who are experts in their relevant disciplines.
We provide comprehensive solutions to every engineering challenge.
